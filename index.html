About Me


My name is Adrian Munoz, I was born and raised is los Angeles CA. I currently live with my little girl in the beautiful high Desert known as Palmdale CA. Growing up I always had a natural talent for drawign and illustrating, at one point I was even privilaged enough to pitch a cartoon to Cartoon Network Studios (it didn't get picked up).

Since then I received my degree in Game Software Development and published my first game in the Play store named Death Bird. I also purchased the Oculus Rift Developers Kit 2 in order to develope my first Virtual reality Game using the Unity 3d engine, in my spare time. For the past two years I've been learning and working with web development. I started off learning the backend first with Ruby on Rails and MVC framework, then learned HTML, CSS and Javascript and currently in the process of earning a Certificate in Angular JS.

I spend my free time with my 11 year old daughter, I just began teaching her the basics of HTML and CSS so we could build a website together. At a young age my father taught me what it means to work hard in the construction field pouring concrete. I am teaching my daughter what it means to work hard and pass down a skill that will help her as an adult like my father did for me
